Delete your account and data

We do not use a separate deletion request. You can delete your account and associated data directly in the app.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the Paylo app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete my account
  4. Confirm in the dialog

Your account and personal data will be permanently removed. Your name will appear as "Deleted member" in any groups you were in. This cannot be undone.

If you no longer have the app or need help, contact us at support@payloapp.com with the subject "Account deletion request".
